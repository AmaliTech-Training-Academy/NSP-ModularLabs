
## Learning Objectives
- Understand the stages involved in the digital product development cycle.  
- Apply knowledge of UI/UX design principles to different stages of development.  
- Analyze the development cycle of an existing digital product.  
- Create a visual representation (flowchart, timeline) of the development process.  

---

## Activity Outlines

### 1. Product Selection
Select a popular application that all group members are familiar with (e.g., Spotify, Netflix, LinkedIn, a university portal, or a fitness tracker).

---

### 2. Identify a Feature Gap
Brainstorm a single, specific new feature that would enhance the app’s value for a defined user segment.

---

### 3. Document the Status Quo
Briefly describe:
- The current application  
- The user segment that will benefit most from the new feature  

---

### 4. Visualization
Create a visual representation of the feature's development cycle using tools like:

#### a. Flowcharts
Showcasing a step-by-step process with clear decision points.

#### b. Timelines
Highlighting the sequence and estimated duration of each development stage.

#### c. Presentations
Combining visuals with explanations to showcase your analysis.

---

## Additional Tips
- You can include additional resources or case studies to showcase real-world examples of feature development cycles.

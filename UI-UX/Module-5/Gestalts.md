
## Learning Objectives
- Comprehend how Gestalt principles influence how users perceive and organize visual elements.  
- Learn how to use Gestalt principles to establish clear visual hierarchy within a design, guiding the user's attention.  
- Apply Gestalt principles to improve the usability and visual appeal of an existing interface.  

---

## Activity Outline

### 1. Product Selection
Refer to the homepage of the provided [design](https://gra.gov.gh/).

---

### 2. Analysis
Analyze the current design and identify areas where Gestalt principles could be better utilized.

Focus on principles such as:
- Proximity  
- Similarity  
- Continuity  
- Closure  
- Figure-ground  

Also consider general design principles where applicable.

---

### 3. Redesign
Recreate the design by incorporating Gestalt principles while maintaining overall design best practices.

Ensure your redesign demonstrates:
- Clear visual hierarchy  
- Improved usability  
- Enhanced visual organization  

---

### 4. Presentation
Present your redesigned interface and explain how Gestalt principles improve the user experience.

---

## NB
- You may use connectors or indicators to show where Gestalt principles were applied.  

---

## Additional Tips
- Emphasize practical application of principles over memorizing definitions.  
- Explore and express your individual style within the framework of Gestalt principles.

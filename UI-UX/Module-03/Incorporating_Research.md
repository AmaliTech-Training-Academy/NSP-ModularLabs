
## Learning Objectives
- Apply research insights to inform information architecture and navigation design.  
- Use research data to justify design choices and communicate them effectively to stakeholders.  
- Integrate research findings into wireframes and prototypes, demonstrating how they address user needs.  
- Prioritize design changes based on the impact and frequency of user issues identified in research.  

---

## Activity Outline

### 1. Design Assumption Validation
Based on your analysis, determine whether the original design assumptions are validated by user feedback.

---

### 2. Incorporate Research into Designs
Integrate research findings into your designs,demonstrating how they address user needs and improve the product based on user insights.

---

### 3. Final Designs
Develop finalized designs that effectively address user requirements.

Ensure you:
- Create and deliver final design iterations  
- Optimize for user satisfaction  
- Clearly show improvements based on research findings
